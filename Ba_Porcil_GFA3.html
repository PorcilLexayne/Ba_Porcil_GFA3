Recognition of Hardwork

Several employees from three companies: Realm of Pastries, Freshly Cooked, and Land of Dough, were invited in the City's Shining Employee Competition. The basis for winning the competition was the time taken for each of the employees to have their company's products be sold out. The employee with the shortest time taken shall be the City's Shining Employee for the year 2023.
